My Journey to Become a Developer

First, let me introduce myself. My name is Juan Garcia, I'm a self-taught full-stack developer with 1 year experience. In that time, I've worked as a front-end developer for a startup called Zyndicate.

This may not sound like a lot of experience, but finishing my first year as a developer has been a huge milestone for me. This is because I had no real experience doing web development - and not much programming experience in general beyond some basic html and css training I got from a few online courses. I also didn't have a computer science degree since I couldn't afford college.

I started working with Amazon without knowing if I would ever get to work as a software engineer. I always felt like I didn't know enough coding to where I could even get a job. Eventually, I heard about Amazon Career Choice. Upon doing research, I found this could be another opportunity to advance in life and get a better chance at securing a job.
